virtual void Glympse::ITicketLite::add15Minutes ( ) [pure virtual]

Adds 15 minutes to ticket remaining time.

Maximum duration of a ticket is 4 hours.

It is not possible to modify tickets that are not yet sent. It is not possible to modify expired tickets.
